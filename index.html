Quality & Characterization Standards

At K.A.T. India, we ensure that every batch of our nanomaterials meets rigorous quality benchmarks. Particle size, morphology, and purity are evaluated using industry-standard techniques such as:

Laser Light Scattering (DLS) – for particle size distribution in liquid suspensions

Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM) and Transmission Electron Microscopy (TEM) – for nanoscale imaging and morphology analysis

X-ray Diffraction (XRD) – to verify crystalline phase and material purity

BET Surface Area Analysis – for measuring specific surface area and assessing particle porosity

These advanced characterization methods help ensure that our materials meet the technical expectations of research and industrial applications alike.
